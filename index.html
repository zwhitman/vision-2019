draft mission

We provide easy access to Census data through our feature-rich API services and intuitive user interfaces that enable our customers to discover, access, and consume our data holdings, while protecting data integrity and maximizing the effectiveness of our resources.

draft vision

We are creating the best data dissemination platform anywhere by delivering a world-class experience for our data consumers, while also enabling our data providers to easily add their data to the platform, and help them optimize their data products to current market demands.
